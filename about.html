Game Design & Development

My first experience in programming and my first legitimate creative venture was Game Design & Development.

This is another discipline I fell in love with during my studies at Bray Institute of Further Education.

During my time at BIFE and onwards, I have so-far made four games and am currently in the process of making three. Over this time I have learned the work that goes into making a project of this nature and the sacrifices and scope you have to make in order to create a successful product.

I have also taken part in a selection of Game Jams both during my time in the United Kingdom and in Ireland. The most notable of which was my participation in the iTag hackathon of 2016 in which me and my team were awarded second place.

Experience

Hackathons

In 2016, I had the opportunity to take part in a Hackathon held by Nottingham Trent University and the University of Nottingham. In this Hackathon, I, along with my team-mates created an Android app that told the user the location of the nearest train station.

My first ever Hackathon was the i<tag> hackathon in 2016. During this event I had my first taste of what a game-jam could be like. Working solidly for 48 hours in a team of strangers to get a working product created. My team placed second

During my time in Bray Institute of Further Education, I was fortunate enough to have my work featured at open-days and general events promoting the course I was on. Examples of this work can be found on the Games section of this website.
